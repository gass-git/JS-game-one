The objective of the game is to make as many lines of the same color as possible. The reward is 10 points per piece.

- Transparent pieces are valid for any color.

- When a bomb impacts, it destroys all sorrounding pieces. The reward is 1 point per piece destroyed.

- The long transparent piece only works when two lines have a matching color.
